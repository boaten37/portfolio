Capstone Project

HW Dental Analytics

MSc Business Analytics · Capstone 2025

Patient Acquisition Optimisation

Multi-branch marketing strategy through data science

In progress
4,546
Patients analysed
4
Branch locations
5
Data dimensions

Problem

HW Dental is experiencing rapid growth across multiple branches but lacks clear visibility into which marketing channels, patient segments, and geographic areas are most effective in driving patient acquisition, leading to potential inefficiencies in marketing strategy.

Data

4,546 new patients across four branches, including demographic, insurance, referral, and geographic information, providing a comprehensive view of patient acquisition patterns.

Journey

Data is cleaned, standardized, and enhanced through feature engineering (age grouping, Medicaid indicator, referral grouping), followed by exploratory and comparative analysis across branches and segments.

Solution

Targeted recommendations to optimize marketing channel allocation and improve outreach strategies, concentrating resources on high-potential geographic areas and patient segments.

Technical Artifacts

Interactive Power BI dashboard visualizing patient acquisition by branch, channel, and segment. R-based exploratory analysis with feature-engineered variables and referral grouping breakdowns.

Impact

Improves acquisition efficiency, enhances demographic targeting, and supports sustainable multi-branch growth through data-driven marketing decisions.
