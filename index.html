Capstone Project

Problem

HW Dental is experiencing rapid growth across multiple branches but lacks clear visibility into which marketing channels, patient segments, and geographic areas are most effective in driving patient acquisition, leading to potential inefficiencies in marketing strategy.

Data

The analysis uses a dataset of 4,546 new patients across four branches, including demographic, insurance, referral, and geographic information, providing a comprehensive view of patient acquisition patterns.

Journey

The data will be cleaned, standardized, and enhanced through feature engineering (e.g., age, Medicaid indicator, referral grouping), followed by exploratory and comparative analysis.

Solution

Targeted recommendations will optimize marketing channel allocation and improve outreach strategies.

Impact

Improves acquisition efficiency, enhances targeting, and supports sustainable growth.
