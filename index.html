About

Bridging impact & analytics

I am a programme and enterprise development professional with a strong foundation in data analytics and business strategy. My experience spans multi-country development programs, cooperative systems, and enterprise advisory, where I have worked closely with women-led businesses, financial institutions, and government stakeholders. Over the years, I have supported the growth of over 10,000 enterprises, managed multimillion-dollar funding programs, and contributed to improving financial inclusion across diverse communities.

Currently pursuing a Master's in Business Analytics, I focus on bridging the gap between data and decision-making. I leverage tools such as Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform complex datasets into actionable insights. My work emphasizes measurable impact, optimizing operations, improving resource allocation, and enabling data-driven growth strategies. I am particularly interested in applying analytics to solve real-world challenges in enterprise systems, financial inclusion, and scalable business models.
